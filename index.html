About

I am a Lecturer in Cyber Security and Software Systems in the School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University. My research interest include security and privacy of graph learning system, AI security and privacy, and LLM safety and security.

Research topics represented in recent work

AI security and privacy
Graph neural network security
LLM safety and unlearning
Backdoor attack detection and mitigation
