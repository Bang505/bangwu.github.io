Research

Research themes

Trustworthy graph neural networks

Security, privacy, robustness, and reliability questions for graph-based learning systems.

Model unlearning and privacy

Methods and attacks related to removing information from trained models and testing what remains recoverable.

AI system security

Defences against training-data misuse, backdoors, jailbreaks, and integrity threats in deployed machine-learning services.
